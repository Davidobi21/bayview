YOUR COMFORT, OUR PRIORITY

Bayview Hotel rooms and suites are designed and decorated to suit its users. Our rooms boast of modern ammenities to boost your comfort and relaxation.

We also provide a high level of privacy and security for our guests.

Bayview Hotel


Satisfying customers is a major key to success. Bayview Hotel is the most preferred hotel in the whole of Lagos and it's all about the right mix. Located in the heart of Victoria Island and shielded from the hustle and bustle of Lagos metropolis, we offer our clients and walk in guests a perfect blend of relaxation, activities and Nigerian tradition delicately infused to meet the hightes international standards.


Overlooking the Eko Atlantic City and Ocean, it is just a 15-minute drive to the City Center and only 45 minutes to the airport (without traffic) and about 3 hours (with traffic). our hotel is designed for your comfort and convenience. Your security and privacy is our primary concern and you'll find our customer care second to none!


Our hotel has taken important steps to live up to West Africa's future model in the hospitality sector. With the best conference and banqueting facilities, tastefully furnished to a 1000-seater capacity, a world-class swimming pool, 224 excellently furnished rooms, mostly with a choice of city and sea views. We have 4 different restaurants and 2 different bars serving a range of international cuisines, amongst other facilities.
