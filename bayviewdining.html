Dining

L'Africana

Situated on the fourth floor of Bayview Hotel, L'Africana restaurant has a 'wow' factor that is hard to dispute. It is about the most classic restaurant in Victoria Island and has a picturesque view of the Eko Atlantic. It offers African fusion menu delicacies. Get pampered by our top chef, Mr Kamsi Onwuka as he perfroms his art with a fusion of culinary traditions. We are open daily for: Lunch: 12noon - 3pm, Dinner: 6pm - 10:30pm, Bar: 12noon - 2am.

Tratorria Italienne

Tratorria Italienne is an Italian fine dining restaurant. Here we take you on a journey of culinary excellence to the taste of the finest Italian cuisines. A subtle blend of classical and jazz music sets the mood at 1415. Our exquisite bar and lounge is designed for the discerning connoisseur of fine wines and spirits. It serves authentic Chinese cuisine formal style. It offers a casual yet all fresco dining. It was dressed to impress you! Opening Hours: Mondays – Sundays from 6am.



Rooftop Restaurant

Located on the pentfloor of Bayview hotel, our rooftop restaurant is the highest restaurant in Lagos. It also has a picturesque view of the city and the Eko Atlantic. It is a place where you can relax in a comfortable and inviting atmosphere. Relish our tender, juicy steaks; mouth-watering chicken; barbeque ribs that fall off the bone and freshest salads. We use the freshest of ingredients and go to great lengths to source the finest of qualities to ensure your utmost pleasure and grilling satisfaction. Opens Every Day for: Breakfast: 6.00am-10.30am, Lunch: 12noon - 3.00pm, Dinner: 6.00pm -10.30pm.

Hookah Lounge

Our hookah lounge and bar is a fun spot to hang out and chill, with good lighting and great music. we serve the best cocktails, daquiris and mojitos. We also serve some mexican dishes such as tacos, enchiladas and burritos. We bring you the perfect Nightlife atmosphere within the confines of the Hotel. Nightlife activities include: Wednesdays: TexMex Night with Unlimited Margaritas, Unlimited Tacos starts 7pm, Thursdays: Snap It, Sip It, Post It Thursdays Starts 7pm,Fridays: Tequi Buzz Friday with Unlimited Tequila (8pm – 11pm). Opening Hours: Monday to Sunday from 6pm.
